Inside Alexa And Carlos PenaVega's Marriage

September 2024 Â· 1 minute read

Celebrities come face to face with the nostalgia craze all the time, and the PenaVegas have definitely been asked about revisiting their most popular roles. Carlos told TV Insider, "Our band Big Time Rush, I would assume at some point we will all come back together and do some sort of reunion. I mean, that's kind of what boy bands do. So I think that that's gonna happen. That's the only thing that could come back."

Alexa was a guest star on "Big Time Rush" in 2013 and isn't opposed to singing with Carlos now. "Listen, never say never, babe. You know it is tough because I love singing. I really, really do, but there is this silly fear, Carlos is so good at singing that I just get so embarrassed. It's something that I have to work up to for sure, but I do love singing, and I do love worship music so you never know, it might happen," Alexa told The War Cry.

On The Sarah Scoop Show, the PenaVegas talked about how much their kids love their work. "However, the greatest feeling is literally having your kid go, 'Mom, I wanna watch 'Spy Kids,' or 'More 'Big Time Rush,'" she shared.
