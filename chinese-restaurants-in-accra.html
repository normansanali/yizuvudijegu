Top 10 Chinese Restaurants In Accra

July 2024 · 4 minute read

Top 10 Chinese Restaurants In Accra – Given that Chinese cuisine is typically highly heavy in salt, this may be the main cause of your body’s yearning for it. When you have a need for salty food, your body is likely dehydrated. Chinese foods may be one of the healthiest go-to cuisines as they mostly contain loads of nutrients, less animal fat, healthy carbs, and fiber.

Chinese cuisine is so diverse that everyone can find something to eat.

If you happen to be curious and want to try out Chinese cuisine in Ghana, these might be the best Chinese restaurants in Accra to visit for a treat;

1. IMPERIAL PEKING CHINESE RESTAURANT

Led by John Chiu, a former chef with experience from Hong Kong and Australia who has trained several Chinese and Ghanaian chefs, to serve you the food at Imperial Peking Restaurant. His culinary team has mastered the art of employing the finest ingredients to provide Imperial’s customers with the delicate flavors of Asia. John is the team’s leader, emphasizing quality, excellence, and gracious hospitality. It is located at Mankata, Accra

2. REGAL CHINESE RESTAURANT

They offer sweet and sour pork, beef with mushrooms and bamboo shoots, spring rolls, fried shrimp noodles, Chinese tea, and excellent customer service. It is located at 10 Dr. Esther Ocloo St, Accra. Phone: +233 30 277 3386.

3. NOBLE HOUSE RESTAURANT- SPINTEX

Fantastic customer service. The well-trained wait staff is always on hand to see if you need any help. Locations, Tema, Tesano, East Legon, Spintex, Osu and Kumasi.

4. PALACE OF THE EAST CHINESE RESTAURANT

Delicious food is served, and the calming atmosphere is complemented by jazz and saxophone music. Contact; +233 30 250 8633. La-Bawaleshi Rd, Accra, Ghana, is the location.

5. JUST CHINESE

Great selection on the menu. An excellent setting for spending time with family and in love. Contact: +233 55 453 0242. Locations: Kofi Annan Street, Accra, Airport, East Legon.

6. BLUE HOUSE- Omanye Street Accra

A hidden gem of a corner restaurant in Osu that specializes in hot pot and grills is situated just near The Shop Accra and Suncity Hotel Apartment.

For those of you who are unfamiliar with hot pot, it is an interactive dinner that involves guests seated around a simmering pot of soup in the middle of the table that contains a variety of raw ingredients, including meat, fish, veggies, tofu, and starches. Being around others makes the experience more enjoyable.

For 188 GHS, Blue House offers a variety of meat options for the hot pot, including beef, lamb, chicken, or pig feet. Then, starting from 20 GHS, you can buy extra hot pot meals including potato, lettuce, tofu, shrimp, and many others.

7. SWEET ROSES 

With a few locations, including Spintex Road, Marina Mall, Palace Mall, Achimota Mall, A&C Mall, Kumasi Brand, and Takoradi Branch, Sweet Roses is primarily known as a “quick food Chinese” choice.

Sichuan pork, which is poached in spicy pig slices, and Ma Po Tofu, which is sautéed tofu in a hot and spicy sauce with the primary components being tofu, minced beef or pork, chilies, and Sichuan pepper, are two delectable dishes from the Sichuan province that this restaurant offers. Sichuan cuisine is notable for using chili peppers, particularly the distinctive Sichuan pepper. Sichuan cuisine meals should be best avoided if you don’t like hot food.

8. SHIFUSUN

This is a wonderful opportunity to try the Friday-only traditional Chinese breakfast. Some of our breakfast suggestions include youtiao, a lengthy golden-brown deep-fried strip of dough, baozi or xiaolongbao, and Jiujiang, a traditional breakfast or brunch in China that is similar to a savory soy milk soup with fried dough (which are both dumplings but with different filings).

This restaurant, which also serves breakfast on Fridays, is renowned for its dumplings and other regional cuisines. They provide beef, pork, and shrimp dumplings, all of which cost between 30 and 35 GHS for 22 pieces. Although the restaurant’s decor may not be the most attractive, the food’s quality and authenticity make up for it, and it’s also extremely reasonably priced compared to other Chinese restaurants. Location: Sixth Ln, Osu.

9. TANG PALACE RESTAURANT

Tang palace deservingly made it to this list for its wide range of Chinese foods and how great they taste. Aside from this, it has great customer service to make customers feel at home. Location: Airport Residential Area. Contact: 020 278 8888

10. HIN LONE CHINESE RESTAURANT

As implied by the name, Hin Lone Chinese Restaurant is a Chinese eatery in Labone, Accra, on Crescent Road. It has simple decor and decent meals to offer. Contact: 030 277 2782.
