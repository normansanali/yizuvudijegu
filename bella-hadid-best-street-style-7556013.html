Bella Hadid's Best-Ever Street Style Outfits

August 2024 · 6 minute read

If I’ve learned anything from working in fashion, it’s this: There’s street style, and then there’s Bella Hadid's street style. It’s kind of like how all thumbs are fingers, but not all fingers are thumbs; the youngest Hadid sister's off-duty aesthetic is a category all its own.

Needless to say, the supermodel has quite the résumé. She has worked with a myriad of designers, from Givenchy to Vivienne Westwood, Tom Ford, and Chanel (to name a few). She’s also been the face of countless campaigns, including ones for Dior and Charlotte Tilbury. Along the way, Hadid has clearly learned styling tips and techniques that have influenced her unique way of dressing. The result? A personal sense of style that's all her own.

Ahead, we’ve rounded up a list of the best Bella Hadid street-style moments from the past few years. Pull up your Pinterest board, these looks are noteworthy.

Decades Dressing

There’s something about the way Bella Hadid dresses that makes the model appear multi-generational. This look is the perfect example of that, with her orange-tinted sunglasses and striped top serving early aughts, brown coat emulating the '70s, and boyfriend jeans giving '90s.

Double-Belting It


Bella doesn’t shy away from mixing and matching the feminine and masculine pieces in her wardrobe. The knit top barring a bit of skin is a stark contrast to the brown leather bomber jacket, pointed-toe shoes, and stacked belts that toughen up the ensemble.  

All of the Denim

Rather than a traditional Canadian tuxedo, Hadid opted for a denim midi dress, and we’re high-key obsessed. The dress features a bib overall-style top with buckle loop fasteners, and she wears a pair of pinstripe bottoms underneath her skirt. She keeps accessories to a minimum, with a long Chanel chain adorned with pearls, a single ring on her left hand, and ballet flats.

A Comfy Groutfit

Don’t be fooled by Bella’s usual street-style uniform; when the model is off-duty, she doesn't snub sweats. Though that doesn’t mean her lax look isn’t any less chic. Hadid makes sure she’s put-together even on her dressed-down days with a monochromatic fit, a sleek, pulled-back hairdo, and stunner shades.

Serious Statement Pieces

Step aside Coastal Cowgirls; for this street-style look, the model went pure Western. We’re not sure what we love more: the gigantic belt buckle at the waist of her midi denim skirt, or the eye-catching checkered duster jacket that seamlessly matches her cherry brown boots. 

All About Abs

This look poses the question: Why choose between the micro crop top and vest-as-shirt trends when you can do both? Though Hadid is showing major midriff with this ensemble, she sustains the professional theme throughout with pin-striped bottoms, dress socks, and loafers.

Ruffled Up

Sure, a lot is going on in this outfit, but riddle us this: Who says chaos can’t be chic? 

Serving Superhero

Leather on leather on leather? Yes, please. 

Going Neutral

A common misconception about monochromatic dressing is that your colors need to perfectly match, but this moment proves monochromatic can also translate to a color scheme. Notice how the different elements of the model’s ensemble belong to the same color family? The shades work together seamlessly, down to the labels on her jacket complementing her heeled boots. 

Sporty Spice

Athleisure doesn’t have to mean workout leggings and a crewneck sweatshirt. Case in point: Hadid’s variation of the aesthetic, complete with a Nike windbreaker and pleated midi skirt. 

Ahead of the Trend

Bermuda shorts are back for summer 2023, and clearly, Hadid was on top of it. Don't think the style is wearable? In baggy white shorts, the model makes a solid argument for giving it a try.

Pattern Mixing

Afraid to mix patterns? Don’t be. Just look to Hadid for inspiration; clearly, Hadid doesn’t shy away from contrast.

Going Green

This writer is a big fan of statement bottoms, especially when they are enhanced with embroidery. Additional snaps for Hadid’s matching footwear; she nailed the entire outfit’s color scheme.

Getting Physical

Athleisure may have gained traction in the 2010s, but Hadid’s spin on the aesthetic channels the '80s. It just goes to show you don’t need leggings and a matching sports bra to look cute when getting active; a pair of shorts and a roomy T-shirt can do the trick, too. 

Full Throwback

Everything about this outfit photographed here screams the '90s. The model has paired two of the alternative era’s biggest trends you wouldn’t usually think to put together — a sheer tube top and swishy low-rise track pants — but somehow she makes it look seamless. 

Elevated Basics

Anyone can sport jeans and a white tee, but if you want to enhance the classic combo, Hadid has some pointers. For starters, swap plain blue jeans for a decorative pair; something with embellishments or a fire graphic (see what we did there?). Then, focus on your accessories. Add outerwear if the weather permits, such as a breezy bomber jacket, and simple jewelry that still makes a statement (i.e. a pair of chunky hoops and sunglasses).

Mad for Plaid

With schoolcore still a major trend, Hadid makes a case for why it should never go out of style: because pieces like a checkered skirt can be seamlessly fused with other aesthetics, like athleisure. 

Wrap Star

This can be found in my "personal favorite" file. You can never go wrong with a wrap dress and calf-high boots. The ensemble will always look sophisticated, and can only be enhanced with accessories. We especially love the model’s stack of dainty gold bracelets, not to mention how her laptop looks just as much a part of the outfit as her mustard shoulder bag.

Late for Gym

Athletic bodysuits are having a moment in 2023. This street-style photo is proof Hadid was way ahead of the trend. It’s giving Cher Horowitz barely “working off the calories in a stick of Carefree Gum," in Clueless.

Party in the Front

Who says blazers have to be all business? Hadid’s sexy contrast of a tailored pinstriped number and a very low-cut bodysuit makes the officewear staple much more playful. Also, note her cheetah print clogs. If you’re not mixing prints, you’re missing out on the fun.

Easy Does It

Last, but certainly not least, is this wonderfully baggy look. Does it look like the model rolled out of bed and shopped her boyfriend's closet? Yes. Does it work? Absolutely.
