Getting Married Completely Changed Jessica Biel And Justin Timberlake's Relationship

July 2024 · 5 minute read

Actress Jessica Biel and husband Justin Timberlake have certainly had their ups and downs as a couple. Through 10 years of marriage, they've experienced it all together, and remain as strong and as committed to each other as ever. The pair reportedly first met formally at the Golden Globes after-party way back in 2007, and got along so well that they sent Justin's ex-girlfriend Cameron Diaz into a rage when she spotted them chatting. Five years later, they married in an idyllic ceremony in Southern Italy, and later welcomed two sons, Silas and Phinnaeus.

Here's the most interesting things Jessica Biel has said about her relationship with hubby Justin Timberlake.

8 Getting Married Completely Changed The Way She Looked At Their Relationship

Its true that marriage changes everything. For Jessica, it was transformative - and for a while, she just reveled in the newness of it.

“It’s so funny — it feels like nothing’s changed, and yet everything’s changed,” Jessica said as a newlywed said on Good Morning America. “It’s kind of that intangible thing. You can’t put your finger on it – I guess the idea of having sort of a partner in crime, and I guess the word ‘husband’ is pretty amazing.”

“I’m loving it,” she added. “It’s so fun to say, ‘Oh, this is my husband.”

7 She Said Their 10 Years Together Have 'Flown By'

Things are so good between them that she can't believe she and Justin have been married so long,

"I sometimes feel like 10 years has just flown by in the blink of an eye and other times I feel like, ‘Oh my gosh, 10 years, that’s a really substantial amount of someone’s life," the Easy Virtue actress said. "I just feel really proud of it. We’ve had our ups and down like everybody. I’m just really proud of it and I’m still the happiest and loving my life.

“It's not always easy, as we all know, but those touch point moments make all the hard times palatable,”

6 Jessica Said They Keep Things Fresh By Keeping Dating

Continue treating somebody how you did in the beginning, and you'll never lose them. This is the secret to Jessica and Justin's relationship success - something the actress says is Justin's idea.

“Well, I’ll have to give Justin the credit in this moment, for this one thing that he always says to me: ‘We might be married, but we have to keep dating,’ and it’s so true.”

5 They Are Always Making Time For Each Other

She continued, “You just have to keep making time for each other, and you have to keep making each other a priority. And do the things that you love together.”

4 Jessica Biel Was Never A Fan Of NSYNC

Jessica claims to have never been a fan of Justin's back in the day when he was part of hugely successful boyband NSYNC. In an awkward interview, the star tried to explain her feelings about the band.

3 It's Not Like She Hated Them Though

Biel explained. "I'm not a huge fan, but I mean, it's cool, I guess okay. That's hilarious because that was by the way not even an answer to a question about NSYNC, no, I mean like, I don't know them, but I kind of know them. I mean I've heard of them. I don't listen to them, but they're fine, they're okay. I didn't say I hate them, no, that would be news-worthy."

2 Their First Kiss Didn't Happen For A Long Time

Surprisingly, it took a long time for Jessica and Justin to share their first kiss - due mostly to being apart for so long.

“We didn’t kiss for a very long time because he was on tour, and we were talking on the phone, like normal pre-teens do,” the actress saId during an interview on The Late Late Show with James Corden. “We were just chatting on the phone for hours. So, we never really kissed for a very long time.”

In a sweet father's day post, Jessica told the world just how patient her man is with her and their boisterous sons.

'Baby, you are the moon, the stars and the sour cream pound cake of my life. You put a roof over our heads, and then fix it when the home made rocket goes terribly wrong. You love the noise and the chaos of it all even though you’ve worked all night are are trying to sleep. We are grateful for you for all the big things and the things we don’t see.
