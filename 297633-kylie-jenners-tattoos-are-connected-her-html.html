Kylie Jenner's Tattoos Are Connected to Her Loved Ones More about Their Meaning

September 2024 · 4 minute read

Kylie Jenner has many small and hidden tattoos all over her body, of which two are names of people dear to her. Meanwhile, Jenner has one matching body art with her former best friend, Jordyn Woods.

Kylie Jenner is not even 30 but has accomplished much more than the average 20-something-year-old. More than her billion-dollar worth, she also has a decent collection of tattoos.

Most of the "Keeping Up With The Kardashian" star's body art are tiny tattoos with special meanings. Of course, one ink is especially dedicated to her firstborn, Stormi.

Kylie Jenner during The 2019 Met Gala Celebrating Camp: Notes on Fashion at Metropolitan Museum of Art on May 06, 2019, in New York City. | Source: Getty Images

Kylie Jenner's "Sanity" Tattoo

Jenner had her first tattoo at 18 years old. She shared a glimpse of the artwork reading "\' sa-ne-tē\" in red ink through an Instagram post in December 2015. Located on the side of her hip, the simple yet precise word was done by tattoo artist Bang Bang.

She later shared the meaning behind her tattoo on Instagram and what it had to do with her anxiety. Jenner said that growing up in the spotlight with prying eyes everywhere was not normal, noting that she sometimes lost herself along the way. Jenner continued:

"My first tattoo was 'sanity' to remind myself every day to keep it."

Kylie Jenner during the 2022 Baby2Baby Gala at Pacific Design Center on November 12, 2022, in West Hollywood, California. | Source: Getty Images

Kylie Jenner's "Before Sanity" Tattoo

In July of the following year, Jenner reportedly added the word "before" to her "sanity" tattoo. Although she hasn't shared a clear snippet of her revamped tattoo, she documented the artwork process by Rafael Valdez.

The Kylie Cosmetics founder has posted photos where the tattoo is seen from a distance, but the exact working remains unclear. The new tattoo now extends further.

Kylie Jenner's "M" Tattoo

Not long after, Jenner came out with another minimalist tattoo on the inside of her pinky finger. A simple letter "m" in a wavy font was inked by Jon Boy, which matches the tattoo of Jordyn Woods.

Kylie Jenner during the 2018 MTV Video Music Awards at Radio City Music Hall on August 20, 2018, in New York City. | Source: Getty Images

Before becoming a reality star, she was a model, etiquette school teacher, and businesswoman, owning Shannon & Company.

Unfortunately, Jenner and Woods are no longer best friends after a falling out when the model kissed Tristan Thompson, Khloe Kardashian's boyfriend. The meaning behind their matching tattoos has never been revealed.

Kylie Jenner's "Stormi" Tattoo

Becoming a mother was one of the most significant moments of Jenner's life. Her love for her firstborn is seen through her action and words, and also a permanent tattoo. Stormi Webster was born in February 2018, and her parents got matching tattoos of her name on her first birthday.

Kylie Jenner and Travis Scott during the 2022 Billboard Music Awards at MGM Grand Garden Arena on May 15, 2022, in Las Vegas, Nevada. | Source: Getty Images

Jenner and Travis Scott trusted Jon Boy to ink Stormi's name in all capital letters. While both tattoos are on the couple's arms, Jenner's is above her right elbow.

Kylie Jenner's "Mary Jo" Tattoo

Besides Stormi, another name inked on Jenner's body is Mary Jo Campbell. Campbell is Jenner's maternal grandmother and someone she trusts with whom she shares a close relationship.

In 2016, the mom-of-two decided to have Campbell's name tattooed on her body in red ink and script font, reportedly her grandmother's handwriting. Campbell, Stormi Webster Jenner's only living great-grandmother, appeared on "Keeping Up With The Kardashians" several times.

Known for her straightforward and dry humor, Campbell has grown to become a beloved family member. Before becoming a reality star, she was a model, etiquette school teacher, and businesswoman, owning Shannon & Company.

Campbell is an empowered woman well-loved by all her grandchildren, including Kim, Kourtney, and Khloe, as seen on their Instagram posts. Her daughter, Kris, also credits Campbell for raising her well, even calling her a hero and best friend.
