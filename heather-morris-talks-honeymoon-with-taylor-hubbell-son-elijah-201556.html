Heather Morris Dishes on Honeymoon With Taylor Hubbell, Son Elijah

July 2024 · 2 minute read

Marriage definitely agrees with Heather Morris! After tying the knot with her childhood sweetheart, Taylor Hubbell, in May 2015, Morris recently honeymooned with her man, and told Us Weekly their getaway was picture-perfect. "We just got back two weeks ago and we had so much fun," Morris told Us of her trip, which she documented on Twitter. "It was so nice to get away and hang out for a week and not have to plan anything. We definitely needed it!"

The couple left their son in the care of their parents during the getaway, but they've had plenty of adventures with him, too. The former Glee star, 28, and her husband say Elijah, 21 months, is experiencing new milestones every day.

"He can count to 10! And he's drawing — he's a smart kid," proud mom Morris told Us at the June 4 premiere of Insidious 3 in L.A. Next up for the first-time parents and their son: Potty training! "I think he'll do pretty good," Morris predicted. "We'll see."

Less than a month after their wedding, the longtime couple is hard-pressed to find a way in which married life differs from when they were dating. "The only difference is the ring. I don't wear jewelry, so that’s an adjustment for me," Hubbell said. "I'm getting used to it."

Agreed his wife: "[Married life] is exactly the same!”

Morris and Hubbell married on May 16 at Old Canyon Ranch in Topanga, Calif., in an outdoor ceremony attended by the bride's Glee costars Naya Rivera, Kevin McHale, Vanessa Lengies, and Jenna Ushkowitz.
