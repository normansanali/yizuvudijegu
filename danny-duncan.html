Danny Duncan Bio, Age, Height, Girlfriend, Net Worth

July 2024 · 5 minute read

Danny Duncan is a popular American YouTuber pursuing acting. He has several videos of him pranking his family, skateboarding, and flaunting the sarcastic humor that has honored him with a $4.5 million net worth. His several hits include ‘Falling with 30,000 Pennies’, ‘Going Down Stairs On A Hoverboard’, and ‘Grocery Shopping With Danny Duncan 4’. His videos get over a million numbers of views and even launched one song along with his own line of merchandise known as “Virginity Rocks”.

Early Life

Danny Duncan was born on 27th of July, 1992 to Sue Duncan who had separated from her husband and Danny’s father. Danny also have siblings in his family, one sister whose name isn’t mentioned by him and one half-brother Mathew. He is raised and based in Englewood, Florida where he attended Bay High School for his graduation. He relishes skateboarding more than anything which can be noticed on his YouTube channel.

Danny Duncan Career

Danny is a prominent YouTuber and is majorly famous for his videos in which he flaunts his sarcastic humor and throws pranks on his family members. Initially, after completing his graduation, he started working at Walgreens but due to a lack of interest, he quit it after a couple of months. Finally, he created his YouTube channel on the 4th of March 2014. During the early stage, he used to upload his skateboarding videos on his channel which landed him an opportunity of training an actor named ‘Jason Lee’ a former skateboarder for a movie. Jason was tempted by Danny’s sarcastic humor and advised him to pursue acting.

Danny started collaborating with his YouTuber friend Christopher Chan for videos that might make his YouTube base strong which would led him to fetch acting gigs. Post this he came up with the idea of posting prank videos which garnered him a huge amount of attention from people by 2016. The most viewed video on his Channel is named ‘Falling with 30,000 Pennis’, which is his first-ever viral video gaining over 29 million views.

He also shows his affection towards his family by featuring them occasionally on his platform. Notably one of his videos is very popular in which he pranks his sister by scaring her by drowning her old car and later surprising her with a new one, and in another video, he surprises his mother by gifting her a new house he bought by his own money he earned through YouTube as a tribute to her because she single-handed raised her children facing many troubles and making numerous sacrifices.

He even tried his hands on music and released a song entitled “I Am Upset” on the 7th of July in 2018 during which his new scooter was stolen. Apart from this his other source of income is considered to be his own line of merchandise named “Virginity Rocks” launched in 2017 bad for which he went on his very first tour starting off in Los Angeles and ending in Chicago while covering numerous other cities including Dallas, Nashville and New York lasting from July to August during 2018.
