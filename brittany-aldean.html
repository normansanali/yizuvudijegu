Brittany Aldean is an American model, singer, professional cheerleader, YouTube star, and social media personality. Brittany Aldean gained popularity after participating in the famous American singing reality show American Idol (season 11). She is also known as the wife of country music star Jason Aldean.

Early Life and Education

Brittany Kerr Aldean was born on 19th June 1988 in her birthplace, North Carolina, the United States of America. Her age is 34 years old, according to her date of birth. She holds American nationality and has a zodiac sign of Gemini.

In addition, she belongs to the Caucasian ethnicity. Moreover, she is the daughter of her father Donald Kerr, and her mother Debbie Kerr.

Talking about her siblings, she has a brother named Hunter Dane Kerr. Her parents raised her and her brother in their hometown.

Talking about her educational qualification, she studied at her local high school in North Carolina. After completing her graduation from high school, she attended the A & M University Normal, Alabama.

She graduated with a degree in Human Environmental Science. In high school time, she was a cheerleader.
