Panda Hidden Among Snowmen Has the Internet Stumped

August 2024 · 2 minute read

There's a panda amongst them! pic.twitter.com/EXxu0PjbCN

We’re still trying to figure it out! A sweet cartoon which depicts a panda hidden among a bunch of snowmen has gone viral just in time for the holiday season, and it seems like everyone on the internet is trying to spot it.

Hungarian cartoonist Gergely Dudas, whose pen name is Dudolf, created the image on Wednesday, December 16, and shared it on his Facebook page. It quickly went viral, gaining more than 51,000 likes and more than 112,000 shares.

The picture has been featured on numerous websites, often accompanied by the tagline, “Can you find the panda?”

Many have taken to Facebook and Twitter to express their bewilderment over not being able to find the hidden animal.

One particular post on Facebook has been shared more than 60,000 times and liked more than 400,000 times.
