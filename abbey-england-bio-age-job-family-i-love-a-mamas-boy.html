Meet Abbey England! Her Bio, Age, Job, Family

September 2024 · 5 minute read

We all have seen overbearing mothers at some point in our lives. But, trust me when I say this none of the ones we have seen are close to the moms of TLC’s hit show I Love A Mama’s Boy. As the third season of I Love A Mama’s Boy returns to TV, we have three new families joining the show. One of them is Tre Welch, his girlfriend Abbey England, and Tre’s overbearing mother Lorenda.

Stick with us as we answer if Tre and Abbey are still together. Also, learn about Abbey England’s kids, age, job, family, and more.

I Love A Mama’s Boy: Are Tre Welch And Abbey England Still Together?

Dating an extreme mama’s boy can be a huge headache for the girl. Season 3 of I Love A Mama’s Boy features 5 young women who vie to become the leading lady in their man’s life. They all are in the hopes that their man will finally cut the cord with their overbearing mother.

Season 3 of the hit TLC show sees the return of Emily, Shekeb, and Mom Laila; Matt, Mom Kelly, and his new girlfriend Brittany. Besides Abbey, Tre, and his mother Lorenda, the other new cast members joining this season are Kristy, Roberto, mama Nancy; Leyna, Ethan, and his mom Esther.

Abbey’s boyfriend, Tre Welch, is a 30-year-old grown man who still can’t live away from his mother Lorenda. In the trailer, Abbey can be seen complaining about how Tre’s mother basically lives in their backyard.

For Lorenda, Tre is still a “baby” and the apple of her eye. Abbey describes the mother-son duo’s relationship as “toxic.” Towards the end of the trailer, Abbey and Tre can be heard having a serious discussion about their relationship and Abbey even warns if Tre doesn’t make some serious changes ASAP in his relationship with Lorenda, their relationship is done for.

As of this writing, it seems like Tre and Abbey have already broken up and aren’t together anymore. Both of their Facebook show single under the Relationship Status section.

Tre was still working as Landscape Architect at Northeast Mississippi Community College as of June 2020. To talk more about him, he previously worked at Walmart. He went to Mississippi State University and Booneville High School for education. Furthermore, he has an Associate’s Degree from Northeast Mississippi Community College.

How Many Kids Does Abbey England Have?

Abbey England shares only one kid with her boyfriend Tre Welch. His name is Kamsyn.

Abbey’s son turned 7 years old on September 7, 2021. On Kam’s 7th birthday, Abbey took to Instagram to share some of his pictures with her from their vacation to Florida and wrote, “7 years with my biggest blessing.”

Kamsyn’s dad also wished his son a happy birthday by making a Facebook post. He wrote, “FINALLY off work to say HAPPY 7th BIRTHDAY to my first born. This guy right here is super smart, always asking WHY, most energetic, athletic, the BEST BIG BROTHER, and daddy’s first…”

Abbey shared on June 2, 2022, on her Facebook that Kamsyn made into Booneville All-Stars and explained how proud she was of her child. She added that unfortunately, Kamsyn had to give up his spot because the game’s date collided with their vacation plans. Nonetheless, she shared that her son would be cheering his team from the beach.

Besides Kamsyn, Tre also has a second child named Noah Thomas Welch. However, we don’t yet if Noah’s mother is Abbey or not.

Recently, Kamsyn’s little brother, Noah Thomas, turned three years old on February 22, 2022. Tre took to Facebook to wish his little one the happiest birthday and expressed his love for his child.

Abbey England Age

Abbey England was 27 years of age as of June 2022.

Abbey England Family

Talking about Abbey England’s family, her parents are Holly Brigman and Todd England. Abbey is equally close to both her parents.

Abbey’s mother, Holly, owns East Side Nutrition and is also a registered Dental Hygienist at Young Dentistry. On Mother’s Day, Abbey wished her mom via Facebook where she wrote, “I wouldn’t make it without this woman… She is the one who picks up where I leave off and reminds me 32 times a day to do things that I still forget to do. She is the best momma and the best Lolly.”

Abbey’s dad is Main Dude at England Custom Tile according to his Facebook. On Father’s Day, Abbey posted some pictures of her dad with her and her son Kamsyn and wrote, “Happy Father’s Day to the very best daddy… Not only is he the best daddy, but he is the very best grandaddy to Kam… We love him more than he will ever know.”

Abbey also has two siblings, a brother, and a sister. They are Colby England and Anna England Chaney. While Colby co-owns East Side Nutrition with his mom, Anna is a Full-Time instructor at the Mississippi State University Department of Interior Design.

Is Abbey England On Instagram?

Abbey England is on Instagram @abbey.england and has a little over 1 thousand followers on the platform. Besides Instagram, she also uses Facebook.

Abbey England Job

Talking about Abbey England’s job, she is an employee at East Side Nutrition. East Side Nutrition basically sells tea and shakes. As Abbey says, she manages East Side Nutrition’s social media pages.

Abbey also works at Jessie K’s boutique store. Jessica K’s sells Men and Women’s Tees, jewelry, Women’s clothes, and Plus Size product.

Abbey England Height

Abbey stands at a below-average height of 5 feet 3 inches.
