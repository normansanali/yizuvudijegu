Jarvis Landry And His Girlfriend Estrella Cerqueira Relationship Story

July 2024 · 6 minute read

Jarvis Landry girlfriend Estrella Cerqueira graduated from the Connecticut School of Broadcasting. Estrella is from Boca Raton, Florida.

Jarvis is a wide receiver for the New Orleans Saints. He has been playing for the team since 2022.

The Miami Dolphins originally drafted him in 2014 as the 2nd rounder. However, he spent only three seasons there before moving to Cleveland Browns, where he stayed till 2021.

In his NFL career, Jarvis has got 7,870 receiving yards in 713 receptions and successfully made 43 touchdowns – 38 receiving and five rushings.

Jarvis has had a great career in the Pro Bowl 5 times and was the NFL Receptions Leader in 2017. He is also honored to be in the 2014 PFWA All-Rookie Team.

Landry played college football for the LSU Tigers from 2011 to 2013.

Jarvis Landry Dating Life

Jarvis and Estrella were first seen together on 8th April 2016.

Estrella posted a picture of them together on Facebook without any captions.

On 1st June 2022, they were seen in Miami Beach, Florida. They enjoyed the boat ride, and Estrella remarked that loyalty is priceless and it is all she needed.

They also took a Puerto Rico trip in July 2022 and had fun on its beach. Estrella expressed on Instagram that the place did not disappoint. 

On Halloween 2021, the couple celebrated the festival together: Landry dressed as Freddy Kruger, while Cerqueira dressed as the Mexican tale’s La Llorona. 

They used to live in Plantation, Florida, during the Dolphins’ career, but have now moved to his hometown of Louisiana after joining the Saints.

Estrella Baby Bump in 2016

Estrella showcased her baby bump for the first time on 5th October 2016. 

She shared her pregnancy and made a caption stating her child kept growing and that she was counting down the days. 

Jarvis’ sweetheart shared another photo at the end of October 2016, displaying her bigger belly, and captioned that her baby was now 31 weeks and growing.

The household conducted a baby shower in their Plantation residence on 30th October 2016. Estrella was eight months pregnant at the time and thanked her sisters, who made her day unforgettable. She also was excited to hold her child in her arms. 

Jarvis and Estrella also celebrated the moment by cutting cakes. Their memorable event was captured by the Women Photograph, a non-profit firm. 

Even though the couple is due for a child, Estrella and Jarvis are yet to become husband and wife.

Estrella Cerqueira Is A Member Of Altitude Group

Estrella Cerqueira is a Data Generation Manager at the Altitude Group. 

She has been involved with the Group since June 2015 and has completed seven years there. Her role in the company is to assist in lead generation for her business clients. Her office is located in her hometown, Bacon Raton. 

Estrella is versatile with her clients, gathering leads for rentals, health insurance, home security, and many more. 

Moreover, she also works as the office manager for the LandFall Data firm. She has been its employee since May 2015. It is an online marketing business that helps various industries in their lead generation and digital advertising.

In her LinkedIn, Estrella has also linked her Altitude Group work by providing her LandFall Data mail address.

Estrella Cerqueira Early Career

Estrella worked as the Sales and Marketing Coordinator for the 12.31 Club. 

She was involved in that organization from August 2013 to August 2014. She had to focus her online marketing and communication expertise on the hospitality industry. 

Estrella also is a former Bartender at Wowie’s Sports Grill. She worked there for two years, i.e. from 2012 – 2014. Her Facebook says she also worked in another bar called Hijinks Sports Grill.

During her academic years, Estrella graduated from the Connecticut School of Broadcasting, where she trained in Radio & Television Production and Broadcasting Technology.

She also studied at Treasure Coast High School in Florida. 

Estrella Is Also A Fitness Trainer

Estrella is a certified personal trainer and loves to show her fitness routines on Instagram. 

She has promoted the Fifth season’s Salads Kit on her IG handle and recommends her 19k followers try out the nutritious diet. 

Estrella also displays her workout routine to her followers to advise people on how to maintain better health. For instance, she made a short Instagram clip saying that she warms up for 15 minutes on the Stairmaster before doing her exercises. 

She also provides advice on how many sets and reps of treadmill exercises, dumbbell lifts, kickbacks, and Glute Bridges people need to do to achieve a superior workout.

Both the kids are living in Louisiana with their dad and mom. They also occasionally travel to Florida, which is their second home.

Estrella also reported that Joy weighed 5 lbs and 5 ounces at birth.

Dietra Landry
