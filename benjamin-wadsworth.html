He played one of the main roles of Joel Jr. in the movie. The same year, Wadsworth also acted in the film called “Psyn: Pseudo Substances Yielding Necrosis” as Cecil / Sam Smith (recurring role).

Moreover, the actor portrayed one of the important roles in the recent project “Deadly Class“. He played the role of Marcus Lopez from 2018 to 2019. Some of his works are Let the Right One In (as Henry), Teen Wolf (as Alec), and Girl Meets World (as Jordan).
