Lana Del Rey buys secluded $3M Malibu beach-front property to escape 'trespassers'

August 2024 Â· 4 minute read

Lana Del Rey is surprisingly elusive for a rock star, even famously trying to back out of a cover story for a 2014 issue of Rolling Stone.

And on Saturday TMZ reported that the 29-year-old has taken another step towards securing her privacy - relocation.

She recently acquired a $3million beachfront property in Malibu, California, as a way to escape overeager fans who could too easily reach her at her current home.

The publication reported that Lana is currently residing in the Hancock Park area of Los Angeles, which, while luxurious, is too accessible to fans.

Fans would reportedly camp out along the street of the Summertime Sadness singer's home, with one even getting into her backyard.

Allegedly the Grammy-nominated performer was tired of having to worry about people encroaching upon her personal space, hence the move.
