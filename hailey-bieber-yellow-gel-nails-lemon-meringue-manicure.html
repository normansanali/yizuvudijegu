Hailey Bieber ignores Selena Gomez drama to start new yellow gel nail trend: lemon meringue manicure

September 2024 · 2 minute read

While the internet can’t get enough of the Selena Gomez vs Hailey Bieber drama, at Hello! Fashion we can’t get enough of Mrs. Bieb’s latest manicure.

Hailey Bieber is the ultimate muse for our nailspiration. No stranger to starting a dessert-based beauty trend, Hailey has graced us with Donut glazed nails, Brownie glazed lips and now she is championing a nail style that is perfect for spring…. The lemon meringue manicure. The Rhode skincare founder is the gift that keeps on giving when it comes to our manis.

Mrs Bieber blessed us all with a series of selfies last night on her Instagram, where she sported pastel pink liner, and (not-so-mellow) yellow gel nails. We’re coining it the Lemon Meringue Mani.

With under three weeks to go until spring officially arrives, the nepo-baby turned supermodel is already trendsetting for the season. We’re expecting to see yellow nails everywhere this season, with Google Searches for ‘yellow gel nails’ already up by 120% over the last day.

Hailey's latest nail trend consists of a bright yellow gel manicure

Want to get Hailey’s look at home? For the ultimate long-lasting mani up to three weeks, use a UV Gel base and top coat. Protect your natural nails and provide a strong and long lasting base for your manicure with the base coat, whilst the top coat helps to shield your manicure from chipping and scratching and get a gorgeous glossy finish.
