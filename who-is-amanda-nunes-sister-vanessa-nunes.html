Amanda Lourenco Nunes is widely considered to be one of if not the greatest female mixed martial artists of all time. 

She competes in the Ultimate Fight Championship, where she is the current UFC Women's Featherweight champion. She is also a two-time UFC Women's Bantamweight champion, and as of August 1, 2022, she is ranked at the top in the UFC Women's pound-for-pound rankings. 

By becoming a featherweight champion after already being the bantamweight champion, Nunes is the first woman to become a two-division UFC champion. She is the third fighter to hold the UFC title in two weight classes simultaneously, along with Irish mixed martial artist Conor McGregor and American former mixed martial artist Daniel Cormier.

Amanda Nunes is also the first and only fighter of either gender to defend two UFC titles while holding both. Nunes is seen as a legend in the sport, not only for competing in both Bantamweight and Featherweight but for her amazing ratio of 22 wins and five losses in a career of 27 matches. 

A legendary 13 of her 22 wins are by knockout, but since she is a black belt in Brazilian jiu-jitsu and judo, it's not a surprise. Nunes continues to terrorize the ring with a reach of 69 inches, height of 173 centimeters, and weight of 61 kgs.

Who Is Vanessa Nunes? Amanda Nunes' Sister

Amanda Nunes and her two older sisters, Valdirene and Vanessa Nunes, were born in Pojuca, Bahia, Brazil.

Her two sisters are not in the public eye, but they love and support their youngest sibling and post about her on their respective Instagram pages. Vanessa especially comes off as a hardcore Amanda Nunes fan page on her Instagram.
