Eva LaRue is a famous American actress and model. Eva LaRue is best known for her work on All My Children as Maria Santos and CSI: Miami as Det. Natalia Boa Vista.

Early life

Eva Maria LaRue best known as Eva LaRue was born on 27th December 1966, in Long Beach, Califonia, the United States of America. As of now, Eva is 57 years old and a citizen of America. Moreover, she has a zodiac sign of Capricorn and belongs to the mixed ethnicity of Puerto Rican-French-English-Dutch-Scottish-German descent.

In addition, she is the daughter of Luis LaRuy (father) and Marcie LaRue (mother). She has three siblings two sisters, Lara LaRue, and Nikka La Rue, and a brother Luis LaRuy Jr.

Eva LaRue – Professional career

In 1987, Eva LaRue made her movie debut as ‘Kara’ in the “sword and sorcery” movie “The Barbarians.” She had her first recurring appearance in the NBC show ‘Santa Barbara the following year. She appears in 24 episodes of the series as ‘Margot Collins.’ Eva appeared in a few films and had cameo appearances in a few TV shows during the next several years. Eva was a TV host on the ‘Candid Camera’ hidden camera reality show. Her first major television appearance was as ‘Dr. Maria Santos Grey’ in the ABC serial opera ‘All My Children,’ which afterward broadcast on ‘The Online Network.’

From 1993 through 2011, she appeared in 129 episodes of the program. There were, however, gaps in between. She was approached to audition for the character of ‘Kendall Hart,’ eventually played by Sarah Michelle Gellar. The casting panel found Eva unfit for the character of ‘Kendall Hart,’ and she was offered the position of ‘Dr. Maria’ about two weeks later.

Television movies

Eva has played in several television movies, including ‘A Dream is a Wish Your Heart Makes: The Annette Funicello Story’ (1995), ‘Remembrance’ (1996), ‘Ice’ (1998), and ”Cries in the Dark” (2006). She has appeared in recurring parts on the NBC crime thriller ‘Third Watch’ (as ‘Brooke,’ the Showtime drama ‘Soul Food’ (as ‘Josefina Alicante,’ and the ABC sitcom ‘George Lopez’ (as ‘Linda Lorenzo’). She made five appearances on the ‘Modern Girl’s Guide to Life’ show on the ‘Style Network’ in 2005.

Appeared in the television series ‘CSI: Miami’

Eva started her second major role in the CBS procedural show ‘CSI: Miami’ the following year. She played ‘Natalia Boa Vista’ in ”CSI: Miami” for 152 episodes from the fifth to the tenth season. In the fourth season, she had a recurrent role. She joined the cast of the comedy ‘Fuller House’ after playing single-episode characters in ‘Criminal Minds’ on CBS and ‘Hell’s Kitchen’ on Fox. In this reboot of the 1987 ‘ABC’ comedy ‘Full House,’ she played ‘Teri Tanner.’ In 2016, the series debuted on Netflix.

Eva has a few significant film credits in addition to her frequent TV appearances, including ‘Ghoulies III: Ghoulies Go to College,’ ‘RoboCop 3,’ and ‘Lakeview Terrace.’ In the 2011 short ‘Grace in Sara,’ she played ‘Dr. Lopez.’ She was the narrator of the documentary ‘World Peace – A Baha’i Vision,’ which was released in 2016.

Other works

Eva has a personal connection to all cancer-related causes because both her grandmother and great-grandmother died of ovarian cancer. She became a spokesman for the ‘National Ovarian Cancer Coalition (NOCC) and developed an educational campaign called ‘Eva’s Divas,’ to educate women about the condition. Moreover, she is also a supporter of the Beckstrand Cancer Foundation, which works to improve the lives of cancer patients and their families.

She is also a businesswoman. ‘Jewel Source’ helped her create her signature jewelry brand, ‘Eva La Rue – Fine Jewelry,’ in 2012. Her designs are largely influenced by the Bahá’ 9-pointed star and reflect spirituality. She has a ‘YouTube’ channel dedicated to parenting-related content.

Eva LaRue – Relationship Status

Currently, this actress is single. But in the past, she was married and divorced three times. First, she married the actor John O’Hurley in 1992 and got divorced after 2 years. After that, she started dating her co-actor, John Callahan. The couple married in 1996. With the marriage, the couple gave birth to Kaya McKenna. But, the couple divorced in 2005. Then, she started to date a businessman, Joseph Cappuccio. The couple married on 26 June 2010 and divorced in early 2014.

In her early years, she was a victim of the casting couch. She previously accused Steven Seagal, an actor, and producer, of sexually harassing her during a screen test.
